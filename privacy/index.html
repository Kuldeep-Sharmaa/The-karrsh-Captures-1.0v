Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Karrsh Captures. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our website, thekarrshcaptures.com, operates and ensures transparency in handling user data.

2. No Collection of Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, or financial information. Visitors can browse our website freely without submitting any personal details.

3. No Use of Cookies or Tracking Technologies

Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel. Your browsing experience remains private and secure.

4. Appointment Booking and Email Communication

Clients can book an appointment through our Contact Page, where inquiries are processed via EmailJS, a secure third-party service.

  • Submitted contact form details are not stored on our website.
  • EmailJS solely facilitates message delivery and does not use the information for any other purpose.
  • No data is used for marketing, analytics, or promotional activities.

Additionally, users can also book directly via WhatsApp using our direct chat option.

For more details on how EmailJS handles data, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

5. No Data Sharing or Third-Party Disclosures

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose user information to third parties. The only third-party service used is EmailJS for message processing. No marketing platforms, analytics tools, or payment processors are connected to our website.

6. Security and Data Protection

Although we do not collect user data, we implement security measures to protect our website's integrity, including:

  • SSL Encryption – Ensuring secure communication.
  • No Data Storage – Eliminating risks of breaches.
  • Third-Party Compliance – EmailJS adheres to strict security protocols.

We advise users to avoid sharing confidential information via the contact form.

7. User Rights and Privacy Control

Since we do not collect personal data, users do not need to request access, modifications, or deletions. If you have privacy concerns regarding our website, feel free to contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review this policy regularly.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
